# ShopWave Customer Support Policies

## Return and Refund Policy
- Standard return window is 30 days from delivery.
- Electronics accessories have an extended 60-day return window.
- High-value electronics have a restricted 15-day return window.
- Refunds are only issued after a successful eligibility check.
- Refund processing time is 5–7 business days once approved.
- Wrong item delivered: If in stock, we offer pickup + replacement. If out of stock, a full refund is issued.
- Damaged/defective on arrival: Requires photo evidence. We offer refund or replacement.

## Cancellation Policy
- Orders in 'processing' status can be cancelled for a full refund.
- Orders in 'shipped' or 'delivered' status cannot be cancelled.

## Exchange Policy
- Exchanges are allowed for wrong size, wrong color, or wrong item delivered.

## Warranty and Escalation
- All warranty claims must be escalated to the specialized Warranty Team.
- Refunds above $200 require supervisor approval and must be escalated.
- Replacement requests for damaged items must be escalated.
- Cases with suspected fraud or conflicting records must be escalated.
- If agent confidence is below 0.6, the case must be escalated.

## Customer Tiers
- VIP Customers: May receive leniency for borderline return window cases, but must be verified in system notes.
- Premium Customers: Borderline judgments allowed with a supervisor note.
